What is Debating?

Debating is a structured argument where two opposing sides present their viewpoints on a topic.

Basic Structure of a Debate

1. **Opening Statement** - Introduce your stance.
2. **Main Arguments** - Present 2-3 strong points with evidence.
3. **Rebuttals** - Counter your opponent’s arguments.
4. **Closing Statement** - Summarize and emphasize your strongest points.

How to Become a Strong Debater

  • 🔹 Research both sides of every debate topic.
  • 🔹 Learn public speaking skills.
  • 🔹 Practice with mock debates.

🇦🇪 Debate & The UAE ▼

The UAE encourages debating through various initiatives:

  • 🏆 **The National Debate Championship** - Annual competition for students.
  • 🌍 **Model United Nations (MUN)** - Debating on international topics.
  • 📖 **Debating in Schools** - Supported by the UAE Ministry of Education.
  • 🎤 **Public Speaking Competitions** - Encouraging confident speakers.
  • 🌏 **Linked to SDG 4 (Quality Education)** - Debate programs enhance critical thinking.
  • 🏗 **Linked to SDG 9 (Innovation & Infrastructure)** - Encourages innovation in education.
