📢 Essential Debate Tips

  • Do Your Research: Always be prepared with strong evidence.
  • Stay Calm and Confident: Confidence makes your argument more convincing.
  • Listen to Opponents: Understanding their arguments helps you counter them.
  • Use Clear Language: Avoid overly complicated words; be direct and persuasive.
  • Structure Your Argument: Use the Claim, Evidence, Reasoning (CER) method.
  • Engage with Emotion: Persuasion is not just logic—show passion for your stance.
