📢 Debate Tips (Click to Expand) ▼
  • 🔹 Structure your speech with an introduction, main points, and a conclusion.
  • 🔹 Always have **evidence** to support your argument.
  • 🔹 Use rhetorical questions to engage your audience.
  • 🔹 Counterarguments make your case stronger.
  • 🔹 Control your voice—speak clearly and confidently.
  • 🔹 Look at your opponent when countering them.
  • 🔹 Never repeat the same point—make new arguments.
  • 🔹 Respect your opponent’s arguments but prove why yours are stronger.
  • 🔹 Start with a powerful statement or statistic.
  • 🔹 Use **real-world examples** to make your case relatable.

🇦🇪 Debate & The UAE ▼

The UAE has a strong debating culture, with initiatives like:

  • 🏆 **The National Debate Championship** - held annually for students across the country.
  • 🌍 **Model United Nations (MUN)** - where students simulate global diplomacy.
  • 📖 **Debating in Schools** - The UAE Ministry of Education promotes debate programs in high schools.
  • 🎤 **Public Speaking Competitions** - Various institutions hold public speaking events to enhance student engagement.
