📚 Sample Debate Topics

  • 🎓 Should school uniforms be mandatory?

    • 🟢 Pro (For Uniforms): "Uniforms create equality and reduce bullying."

    🔴 Con (Against Uniforms): "But they limit self-expression and force students into conformity."

    🟢 Pro: "It also makes mornings easier—no need to decide what to wear!"

    🔴 Con: "But some parents can't afford extra uniforms. That creates new problems."

    🟢 Pro: "Studies show that school performance improves when students wear uniforms."

    🔴 Con: "But uniforms don't stop bullying completely. Kids can still find other ways to judge."

  • 🤖 Will AI take over jobs?

    • 🟢 Pro (AI Will Replace Jobs): "Automation is reducing jobs in many industries."

    🔴 Con (AI Won’t Replace Jobs): "AI also creates new job opportunities in tech fields."

    🟢 Pro: "Yet, not everyone can suddenly become a programmer. What happens to those workers?"

    🔴 Con: "We must focus on retraining workers instead of fearing AI."
