🔥 Essential Debate Tips

Master the art of debate with these expert tips!

  • Use the "Rule of Three" 🏅 - Always have 3 strong points.
  • Counter Your Opponent 🥊 - Always address their main argument.
  • Be Concise ✨ - The best debaters say the most with the fewest words.
  • Use Rhetorical Questions 🤔 - Challenge your opponent’s ideas with powerful questions.
  • End with a Strong Conclusion 🎯 - Leave the judges and audience thinking.
