🔥 Essential Debate Tips

  • 📌 **The Power of Rebuttals**

    • A strong rebuttal can destroy your opponent’s argument. Always listen carefully and **find weaknesses** in their logic.

  • 🗣️ **Body Language Matters**

    • Confident body language makes you look more convincing. Stand up straight, **make eye contact**, and use hand gestures.

  • 🧐 **Use Evidence & Statistics**

    • Back up your claims with real data! Statistics make your argument stronger and more reliable.
