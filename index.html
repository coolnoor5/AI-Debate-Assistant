🔥 Debate Tips

  • 📝 **Structure Your Argument**

    • Start with a strong opening, present key points, and end with a solid conclusion.

  • 📊 **Use Strong Evidence**

    • Use statistics, facts, and expert opinions to support your claims.

  • 🎙️ **Speak Clearly & Confidently**

    • Maintain eye contact, use clear pronunciation, and control your tone.

  • 👂 **Listen to Your Opponent**

    • A strong debater doesn't just talk—they also listen and respond effectively.
