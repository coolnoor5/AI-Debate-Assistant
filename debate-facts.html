🎉 Fun Debate Facts!

  • 🗣️ The first debates were held in **Ancient Greece**, over 2,400 years ago!
  • 🏆 Many famous world leaders started as **debate champions**.
  • 🎤 Some professional debaters **earn money** by competing in tournaments.
  • ⚖️ The **Lincoln-Douglas debates (1858)** were so famous that they are still studied today!
  • 🌎 Debate competitions are held **worldwide**, from the USA to Japan to South Africa!
